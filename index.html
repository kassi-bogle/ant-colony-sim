Install Throughput

Rack placement: 45 labor hrs/row × 160 rows = 7,200 hrs
7 days = 7,200 / (HC × 10 useful hrs × 2 shifts)
HC = 7,200 / 140 = 52 people/shift

Cabling (L11): ~5 hrs/row × 160 = 800 hrs
6 days = 800 / (HC × 10 × 2)
HC = 800 / 120 = 7 people/shift
V7 baseline: CB3 used 20 HC/shift → 14.4 days for 8 pods. We need 52 HC/shift (2.6× crew) for 10 pods in 7 days (2.6× faster).

Door Placement — South Wall

Interior Logistics — Dock to Row

Delivery Path

  1. Truck unloads at south wall dock bay (forklift → staging lane)
  2. Material handlers stage by row assignment
  3. Tugger train (4–6 racks/trip) enters DH via south access corridor
  4. Tugger proceeds down center aisle to target row
  5. Pallet jack places individual racks at position
  6. Empty tugger returns to staging for next load

Travel Distance

Max one-way: ~275' (door to far row)
Average one-way: ~200'
Round trip: ~400'
At tugger speed (5 mph): <1 min travel

Acceleration Options (If Needed)

Option 1: Delay admin interior build-out
Leave admin block (cols 30–41) as open staging during sprint. Gain ~20,000 sq ft. Admin runs from trailers. Saves 1–2 days by eliminating staging bottleneck.
Option 2: South wall knock-out panels
Design 2–4 removable wall sections → 10–12 total dock positions during sprint. Seal post-sprint. Increases capacity from 96 to 120–144 TL/day.
Option 3: Skip pod partition walls initially
Wider aisles, more flexible tugger routing. Build walls after rack placement. Saves ~0.5–1 day on install.
Option 4: North wall temporary access (CU bay knock-outs)
Open 2–4 CU bay positions for direct DH access from north. Doubles interior delivery paths. Most aggressive — saves 1–2 days but adds rework cost.

Key Assumptions

  • Dock cycle: 1.5 hrs/truck unload (per v7 CB3 throughput model)
  • Shift model: 2×12hr shifts, 24/7 coverage, 75% worker utilization (per v7)
  • Rack placement labor: 45 hrs/row (per v7 — Bloom GA Deployment Playbook)
  • Rack cabling labor: ~5 hrs/row (L11 pre-cabled state eliminates v7's 27 hrs/row)
  • Row constraint: Max 3 workers per row space (per v7 CB3 deployment plan)
  • Rack spec: TPU v8 — 28" wide × 48" deep, L11 state (pre-cabled from factory)
  • Truck capacity: ~20 racks/truck (weight-limited at 44,000 lb payload)
  • V7 inbound rate: 5 TL/shift at CB3 was a supply constraint, not dock capacity
  • Excluded from critical path: CDU installation, machine population (per user direction)
  • QA/turn-up: Not included in 7-day target — follows physical placement
  • Pre-staging: Recommended but not required with 8 doors (vendor schedule dependent)
